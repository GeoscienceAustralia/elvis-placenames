Need help on how to search?
Searching is conducted on the current map view. Pan and zoom the map to your area of interest

You can apply filters for:
Features matching on partial or like name, groups, categories and features.
You can restrict results to only authorities of interest.

Once you have zoomed, panned and filtered to your desired results hit the search button to list details with the option to download in a variety of projections and formats.

NB Name searching is done on "fuzzy" searching which means it isn't always an exact match but a match something like what is typed.
If you are interested in features in the Antarctic consider using the search specific to the polar view
